Logic & Formula

An LED needs a current-limiting resistor to prevent burnout. The resistor drops the excess voltage.

R = Vs − (Vf × n)If

Where Vs = source voltage, Vf = LED forward voltage, n = number of LEDs in series, If = desired current.
