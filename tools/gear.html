Gear Ratio

Logic & Formula

The gear ratio is determined by the number of teeth on each gear. It defines how speed and torque are transferred between meshed gears.

Ratio = Driven TeethDriving Teeth RPMout = RPMin / Ratio Torqueout = Torquein × Ratio
