What is Pi?

π (Pi)

The Circle Constant

The ratio of any circle's circumference to its diameter. Regardless of size, this ratio is always π ≈ 3.14159...

If diameter = 1, then the perimeter = π

An irrational number — its decimals never end or repeat. Ancient mathematicians discovered this constant appears universally in all circles, nature, and physics.
